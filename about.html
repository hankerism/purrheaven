About Purr Heaven

A Cozy Place Where Every Cat Gets a Second Chance

Purr Heaven began with a spare room, three rescued kittens, and one stubborn belief: every cat deserves warmth, patience, and a place to call home.

Our Mission

Warm homes, never cages

We exist to give rescued cats the time and tenderness they need — then to match each one with a family for life.

  • Rescue with care

    We take cats in from shelters, streets and surrenders — no judgement, no waiting list.

  • Heal, never rush

    Every cat gets the medicine, space and patience they need before they're ready to meet anyone.

  • Match for life

    We rehome thoughtfully, so each adoption is the last home a cat will ever need.

Our Rescue Journey

How a spare room became a sanctuary

What started as one person fostering kittens grew, year by year, into a cage-free café and adoption home for hundreds of cats.

  1. 2018

    A spare room & three kittens

    Abby fosters her first rescues at home, and the idea for a kinder kind of shelter is born.

  2. 2020

    Purr Heaven opens its doors

    Our cage-free café and adoption lounge welcomes its very first guests — and its first residents.

  3. 2022

    Our 1,000th forever home

    A senior tabby named Biscuit becomes our thousandth adoption, dozing all the way home.

  4. 2025

    A bigger, brighter lounge

    We expand the sunroom and launch a city-wide foster network to reach even more cats in need.

What Makes Us Special

Adoptions and coffee, with a conscience

  • A cage-free lounge

    Cats roam, nap and play freely — no bars, no stress, just a real home to relax in.

  • Coffee that gives back

    Every café visit helps fund food, medicine and warm beds for cats still waiting.

  • Support that doesn't stop

    A free first vet check and lifetime guidance come with every single adoption.
