Our process is simple, supportive, and built around one thing: finding the right match for you and for the cat. Here's how it works.

The Journey

From first hello to forever home

1Step 01

2Step 02

Submit an application

Tell us about you and your home with a short, friendly form. No essays — just enough for us to make a match that lasts.

Meet your match

Good to Know

Adoption questions, answered

How long does the process take?+

Usually one to two weeks from application to adoption day, depending on home checks and finding the right match.

What does the adoption fee cover?+

Vaccinations, microchipping, neutering and your cat's first vet check — the essentials, already taken care of.

Can I adopt if I rent?+

Absolutely — we just ask for confirmation that your landlord allows cats.

Do you adopt to homes with kids or other pets?+

Often, yes. We'll talk through introductions together to set everyone up for success.

What if it doesn't work out?+

Life happens. If things change, our cats are always welcome back — no judgement, ever.

Do I need to visit in person first?+

A lounge visit is the best way to meet your match, but we're happy to start the conversation online.

Ready to meet your new best friend?

Start your application today, or come browse the cats in person. Either way, a warm welcome (and a few purrs) await.
